Project Description

The homeowners in Centurion approached us with a common challenge: their kitchen was dated, cramped, and separated from the main living area. Their goal was to create a bright, modern, and open-plan hub for their family that was both beautiful and highly functional for daily life and entertaining.

The Terraformer team managed the entire project from start to finish. Our process began with a complete demolition and structural assessment. We then rerouted essential plumbing and electrical lines to accommodate a new, large central island. We worked closely with the client to select high-quality materials, from the durable porcelain floor tiles to the custom-built, two-tone cabinetry and elegant quartz countertops, ensuring every detail aligned with their vision.

  • Complete redesign for an open-plan layout.
  • Installation of new, energy-efficient LED lighting.
  • Custom-built central island with quartz countertops.
  • All work completed on schedule and within budget.
  • Licensed Plumbing & Electrical work.
  • Professional Floor and Wall Tiling.
  • Custom Cabinetry Installation.
